Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for film production?

We operate with a budget of $0. We create films out of passion, not profit, and never ask for money from our community.

What types of games do you adapt?

We focus on games with rich lore and compelling narratives. We stay authentic to the source material while bringing our cinematic vision to the story.

How can I propose a game for adaptation?

Join our Discord server and reach out to our team. We're always interested in hearing about games with great storytelling potential.

Can I join your production team?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate creators. Check out our careers page for current opportunities.
