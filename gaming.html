Our Production Process

From your game concept to cinematic masterpiece in Roblox

1

Project Submission

Submit your game for consideration through our contact form. We review each submission to ensure it aligns with our production capabilities and storytelling vision.

2

Requirements Gathering

The most we will need from you is:

  • The map from your game
  • Any major systems from the game
  • You, so we can ask questions about the game periodically!
3

Script & Pre-Production

Our team develops a cinematic script that captures the essence of your game. We focus on drama-driven narratives that honor the source material while creating compelling viewing experiences.

4

Roblox Production

Using cutting-edge Roblox filmmaking techniques, we bring your game to life with cinematic quality production values. Our voluntary team of passionate creators handles all aspects of production.

5

Release & Distribution

Your completed film is released on our platforms and made available to the gaming community. We handle all distribution while ensuring you receive full credit as the original game creator.
